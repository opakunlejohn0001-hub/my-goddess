To My Goddess ❤️

A Letter From My Heart

A memory I will always treasure. ❤️

My Goddess,

There is something I've always wanted you to know.

Do you know why I call you my Goddess?

It is because I think you're extraordinary. It is because I cherish you above everyone else. From the day I truly got to know you, I discovered a woman whose character, discipline, kindness, gentleness, and love completely captured my heart.

Yesterday made me realize even more how much I value you.

I know I hurt you, and I'm truly sorry for that. Seeing your pain hurt me too, because the last thing I ever wanted was to make you uncomfortable or make you feel unsafe with me.

What touched my heart wasn't what happened physically—it was the trust you placed in me and how sincere you have always been with me.

It made me trust you more.

It made me love you more.

It made me care about you more.

It made me cherish you more.

You are a woman of conviction, discipline, integrity, and a heart so full of love.

When I call you my Goddess, it's because I see someone incredibly precious—someone I treasure deeply and thank God for every single day.

I know I've made mistakes, but one thing I never want you to question is how much I love you.

I can't hide it anymore.

I love you with all my heart.

I know the beginning of our journey has been rough, but I believe that one day we'll tell people our love story, and they'll smile because they'll see that true love is about choosing each other, growing together, forgiving each other, and never giving up.

You are my perfect woman.

Thank you, my Goddess, my answered prayer, my peace, my love, and the woman I hope to spend the rest of my life with.

I love you more deeply than words can ever express.

No matter how many chapters our story has, I will never get tired of writing "I love you" in every single one.

Thank you for being my Goddess.

I Love You Endlessly ❤️

Forever Yours,
With all my heart ❤️
